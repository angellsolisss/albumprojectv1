Personal Notes

Is This It sounds like what I would imagine it's like to be a twenty-something-year-old in New York in the early 2000s, it sounds so young and alive with a hint of douchiness thanks to Julian Casablancas' song writing and vocal performances. Almost every song is about the vapidest, shallow problems that only people like The Strokes have to worry about like lying about your interests to get someone's apartment or New York City cops being "not so smart" (The track New York City Cops isn't on this ranking since it was removed from major releases of the album) and yet it all sounds so relatable. I can listen to these douchebags sing about their problems and have a good time which is a quality that I haven't heard from any other band, and for that, these skinny jean-wearing goons take the second spot on my list.

Overall Score: 10/10
