Personal Notes

Daft Punk has always and will always hold a special place in my heart, since I was a kid I remember being infatuated with the French duo and their aesthetic that they crafted expertly. From seeing cringy finger dancing videos on Youtube in 2007 set to Harder, Better, Faster, Stronger to Interstella 5555 which is an animated movie that accompanies this album, I will always feel a warm sense of nostalgia whenever thinking about Daft Punk and this album. When thinking about the other albums on this list, you would most likely be able to give a rough guess as to when they were released, but Discovery is so timeless that it truly fits in and could pass as an album that came out in either the 80s or some time in the distant future. The mix of groovy basslines and futuristic synth patterns is what makes Discovery such a joy to listen to each and every time.

Overall Score: 9/10
