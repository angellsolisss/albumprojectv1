Personal Notes

This album has been my number one for as long as I've known about The Cure. Everything about Robert Smith's unique vocals that sound whiny but in a way that sounds like he's calling for help, to the bleak whirr of the guitars make this album awesome to me. What makes it number one among the other fantastic albums on this list is just how perfectly Smith and company immerse you in the soundscape that they built, the album cover fits the sound perfectly, the second you start listening to Seventeen Seconds you are transported to a cold, desolate forest with no one around to help and now you have no other option but to listen to this British man with silly hair sing about the most depressing things imaginable.

Overall Score: 10/10
